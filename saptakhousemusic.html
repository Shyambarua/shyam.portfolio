About Us

Born and raised on the mean streets of AGRA, DJ DEMOXIT, AKA SHYAM BARUA, started his musical journey in 2019. It was at this moment that SHYAM BARUA knew he wanted to be part of 'The Scene'. He first became interested in the electronic music scene in 2020. He did various warm up sets before the doors opened in clubs hosting major named DJ's. In 2021, he achieved his big break when he a competition on an EDM forum to play a set at Sunburn Academy, which DJ DEMOXIT described as the greatest night of his life. It was after this point when he started getting gigs at big events such as KIITFEST and CLUBS. After receiving several offers, DJ DEMOXIT Started SAPTAK HOUSE MUSIC, and produced his first internet release, 'LIFE IS A HIP HOP' in 2021. Though the single didn't sell as many as he probably would have liked, DJ DEMOXIT is a fighter and since 2022 has been locked in the studio perfecting his craft.
